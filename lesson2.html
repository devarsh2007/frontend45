The short story is a crafted form in its own right. Short stories make use of plot, resonance and other dynamic components as in a novel, but typically to a lesser degree.

While the short story is largely distinct from the novel or novella/short novel, authors generally draw from a common pool of literary techniques.
[citation needed] The short story is sometimes referred to as a genre.[2]
